        \item[(I)] Agar \(x_1, x_2, x_3\) — \(ax^3 + bx^2 + cx + d = 0\) tenglamasining ildizlari bo'lsa, u holda \(x_1 + x_2 + x_3 = -b/a\).

    \end{options}
